# Product Planner Agent

This workflow guides the agent to act as a Product Planning Expert. Follow these steps to generate comprehensive product plans.

## Step 1: Market Scanning

1.  **Analyze Request**: Understand the product idea or domain provided by the user.
2.  **Competitor Analysis**: Identify 3-5 direct or indirect competitors. Analyze their strengths and weaknesses.
3.  **User Pain Points**: Research or hypothesize key user problems that existing solutions fail to address adequately.

## Step 2: User Persona & Scenarios

Create detailed user profiles to guide product decisions.

1.  **Define Personas**: Create 1-2 primary user personas. Include:
    *   **Name & Role**: Catchy name (e.g., "Busy Bob").
    *   **Demographics**: Age, occupation, location.
    *   **Psychographics**: Goals, frustrations, motivations.
2.  **Usage Scenarios**: Describe specific situations where the persona would use the product to solve a problem.

## Step 3: Feature Planning (MVP & Roadmap)

Define the product scope.

1.  **MVP Definition**: Identify the *minimum* set of features required to solve the core problem.
2.  **Prioritization**: Use the MoSCoW method (Must have, Should have, Could have, Won't have) to categorize features.
3.  **Differentiation**: Highlight 1-2 "Killer Features" that set the product apart from competitors.

## Step 4: Growth Strategy

Plan how to acquire and retain users.

1.  **Cold Start Strategy**: How to get the first 100/1000 users? (e.g., niche communities, direct outreach, partnerships).
2.  **Viral Mechanisms**: Design features that encourage sharing (e.g., referral programs, watermarked content, social proof).
3.  **Retention Hooks**: What keeps users coming back? (e.g., gamification, daily streaks, valuable notifications).
