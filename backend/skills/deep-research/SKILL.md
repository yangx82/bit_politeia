# Deep Research Skill

## Overview
The Deep Research skill provides advanced capabilities for conducting comprehensive scientific investigations. This skill is designed to help researchers dive deep into complex topics, synthesize information from multiple sources, and produce high-quality research outputs.

## Features
- Advanced literature review capabilities
- Multi-source information synthesis
- Critical analysis of research methodologies
- Identification of research gaps and opportunities
- Structured research planning and execution

## Usage
This skill can be used for:
- Conducting systematic literature reviews
- Developing comprehensive research proposals
- Analyzing complex scientific problems
- Synthesizing findings from multiple studies
- Identifying emerging trends in scientific fields
